Post Traumatic Stress Disorder Test
1. Have you experienced, witnessed, or heard from a person close to you (family or friend) about a threatening event? Examples include actual or threatened death, serious injury, or sexual violence.
2. Since this event occurred, have you experienced recurrent involuntary memories or dreams that make you suffer?
3. Do you become distressed by sights, sounds, smells or feelings that remind you of the traumatic event?
4. Do you try not to think about the traumatic event?
5. Do you try not to see people, go to places or see objects that are associated with the traumatic event?
6. Do you have trouble remembering an important aspect of the traumatic event?
7. Because of the event, do you now believe that no one can be trusted?
8. Do you blame yourself for the event happening?
9. Do you feel fear, guilt or shame about the traumatic event?
10. Are you uninterested in activities you enjoyed before the traumatic event?
11. Do you feel disconnected from people?
12. Is it very difficult for you to feel happiness, satisfaction or love?
13. Are you feeling angrier than before the traumatic event occurred?
