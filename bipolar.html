Bipolar Disorder Test
Has there ever been a period of time when you were not your usual self and...
1. You felt so good or hyper that other people thought you were not your normal self or were so hyper that you got into trouble?
2. You were so irritable that you shouted at people or started fights or arguments?
3. You got much less sleep than usual and found you didn't really miss it?
4. You were much more talkative or spoke much faster than usual?
5. Thoughts raced through your head or you couldn't slow your mind down?
6. You were so easily distracted by things around you that you had trouble concentrating or staying on track?
7. You had much more energy than usual?
8. You were much more social or outgoing than usual, for example, you telephoned friends in the middle of the night?
9. You did things that were unusual for you or that other people might have thought were excessive, foolish, or risky?
10. Have any of your blood relatives (Children, siblings, parents, grandparents, aunts, and uncles) had manic-depressive illness or bipolar disorder?
